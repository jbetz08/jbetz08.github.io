Target Audience:

Our target audience for "Southeast Idaho Weather" are citizens in Rexburg, Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, Pocatello, and Blackfoot. People who are interested in current weather, and the weather forcast in their area. My goals with this site are to inform the users on various weather patterns in the area.

Persona 1:

A user that freqently checks the weather and weather patterns to determine what outdoor activities they can participate in that day.

Persona 2:

A user that commutes to work during the week day and needs to prepare for road conditions.

Scenarios:

  • A user checking the snowfall at or near a ski resort to determine if they should go skiing or not
  • A user planning a road trip, and needs to know if they should put their snow tires on their car or not
  • A user planning a hike with friends and needs to know if it will be wet or dry out
  • A commuter needing to know how the weather will affect their daily commute
  • A baseball player wanting to know how the wind will affect the game
